# choicer: Discrete Choice Models for Economic Applications

Fast estimation of discrete-choice models for applied economics.
Likelihoods, analytical gradients and Hessians are implemented in C++
with 'OpenMP' parallelism, scaling efficiently to specifications with
many alternative-specific constants. Post-estimation routines return
predicted shares, own- and cross-price elasticities, and diversion
ratios. Supports multinomial logit ('MNL'), mixed logit ('MXL'), and
nested logit ('NL').
